About Us

Our Story

Shri Lakshmi Foods brings you the original cuisine from the North of India. The original cuisine from the North of India is vegetarian, vegan and plant based. In this era, the early years of the 21st century, modern society has realised the value of vegan and vegetarian food. With the advent of social media, modern society has begun to recognise that the consumptiuon of vegan and vegetarian food is essential as we are also moving toward becoming a more humane society and all life on this planet, including the lives of animals, is important. Thus this massive move towards creating new and exciting tastes has produced new styles of cooking utilising plant based ingredients in order to create new healthy and tasty dishes that can be enjoyed by one and all without sacrificing on nutrition.

Well, the Vegan and Vegetarian food of North India has been feeding generations of people for a few thousand years. It is tasty, nutritious and does not require any form of processing. The ingredients are vegetables, the preparation requires herbs and spices from India and many scientists today have extolled the value of these herbs, for example, turmeric has been proven to carry medicinal qualities.
